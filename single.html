Founder & CEO Of Uzuri Congo Tours

UZURI CONGO TOURS is a tour company based in the Democratic Republic Of Congo since the last year 2023 and it mission is to promote the culture and wildlife tourism industry in the DRC throught the community based tourism working with the local people around the protectected ereas and also involved in the conservation. I have got this inspiration from Red Rocks Initiative For Sustainable Development in Rwanda where i was able to learn about the tourism industry in general. So then i was back in my home country and i started to build the same project and decided to launch it in november 2023. Curently according to the security issues in the Eastern CONGO the project is facing a lot of challenges because of most of our operation areas are locked due to the conflict between the congolese government and the M23 rebels. The goal of this project is to ensure that the beauty of the congolese culture is promoted to the international tourism market and of course make it benefic for the local community around the virunga national parc. therefore, at the same time we create conservation projects and protect the environment. I invite you to join us and work together if interested. to see more about UZURI CONGO TOURS don't hesitate to visite our website (www.uzuricongotours.org) or social medias to keep in touch.
